Cocktail 1 Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 oz (60 ml) Tequila
  • 1 oz (30 ml) Triple sec (orange liqueur)
  • 1 oz (30 ml) Freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1/2 oz (15 ml) Simple syrup (1:1 water and sugar)
  • Ice
  • Salt (for rimming the glass)
  • Lime wedge for garnish

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Rim the edge of a chilled glass with salt by moistening the rim with a lime wedge and dipping it in salt
  2. Step 2: Fill a shaker with ice, then add the tequila, triple sec, lime juice, and simple syrup
  3. Step 3: Shake well until chilled, then strain the mixture into the prepared glass filled with ice
  4. Step 4 : Garnish with a lime wedge, and enjoy your classic Margarita!
