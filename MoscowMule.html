Cocktail 1 Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 oz (60 ml) Vodka
  • 1/2 oz (15 ml) Freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 4 oz (120 ml) Ginger beer
  • 1/2 oz (15 ml) Simple syrup (1:1 water and sugar)
  • Ice
  • Lime wheel or wedge for garnish
  • Copper mug (traditional, but not required)

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Fill a copper mug (or a highball glass) with ice.
  2. Step 2: 3 oz (90 ml) Pineapple juice
  3. Step 3: 1 oz (30 ml) Coconut cream
  4. Step 4 : 1 cup Ice
